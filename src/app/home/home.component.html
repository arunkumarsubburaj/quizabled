About

Quizabled is a distinctive quizzing program for children and Youth who are Differently Abled which is organized by Seva-in-Action, Bengaluru (S.i.A) and powered by Larsen and Toubro InfoTech Ltd (LTI). The event is designed to include five different categories of disability namely, Intellectual Disability (ID), Cerebral Palsy (CP), Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), Visual Impairment (VI), and Hearing Impairment (HI).

Genesis

Quizabled was conceptualized by Seva-in-Action in the year 2016 and was curated by 4 Edge Quizzing Solutions. The concept became a reality with LTI powering it since its inception. It is first of its kind in India, where children and youth with disabilities are given an opportunity to showcase knowledge and intellectual capabilities. The main purpose of this program is to provide a platform for the differently abled for knowledge development, to raise awareness by dispelling myths about the potentials of children & youth with intellectual and developmental disabilities, and to promote and encourage participation of the differently abled in quizzing events and even take part in competitive exams in the future. With the event being curated and conducted by Professional Quiz Masters, the program offers a very positive and professional environment, thereby creating interest for information gathering amongst participants and ensuring that their quest for knowledge never ends.

The initial Editions of ‘Quizabled’ were held physically at Bengaluru, Karnataka and in the year ‘2018’ it was expanded to other States in collaboration with ‘The Spastics Society of TamilNadu(SPASTN)’ –Chennai and in collaboration with ‘Sujaya Foundation’, Mumbai, Maharashtra. The physical event saw more than 2000 students and Youth with disabilities take part in it.

In the course of this Pandemic Era, ‘Quizabled’ was launched on a virtual platform in the year, ‘2021 -22’ to keep up with the new normal. By launching the event online, it has opened up a series of positive collaborations with our existing/new stakeholders, Corporate & NGO partners and also ascertaining that the interest and enthusiasm of the participants never faded.

While we were met with several barriers and challenges, the response has been overwhelming so far. Nearly, 500 children/youth with disabilities across Karnataka, Mumbai and TamilNadu took part in the First Virtual event (2020-21) with great enthusiasm. This has indeed prompted us to make further modifications to the online event and also reach out to other Organisations and Schools across new districts of Karnataka, TamilNadu in collaboration with our NGO Partner Organisations.

This time, as ‘Quizabled’ steps into its 7th year, we now once again have the opportunity to explore the potentials of the digital platform and engage with many more children and Youth with disabilities. This year, a total number of 13 districts from Karnataka, 4 new districts from Maharashtra and 12 districts from TamilNadu have been engaged with. An expected number of 1150 participants are all set to take part in ‘Quizabled 2021-22’ on a virtual mode.

We believe that events like ‘Quizabled’ are an integral part of the lives of every person for it changes and shapes the very basic attitudes that much needed to create an Inclusive Society. In line with our Mission statements, we are determined to take “Quizabled” to the next level. Way forward, we aim at making this platform an opportunity to disseminate and to showcase our approaches towards bringing about transformative changes in the society towards Inclusion, Advocacy and also Influence Policy Change.

For more information , visit @www.sevainaction.org

Rules & Eligiblity

Quizabled will be organised into two parts: Online Quiz and Virtual Quiz. Contestants will be eligible for one or the other based on the disability category that they belong to.

Take the quiz online!

The quiz portal will be open from 10am to 10pm on quiz day. The participants can take it at anytime by visiting the quiz portal and logging in using the login ID and password which will be provided after registration.

The quiz will be consist of 20 multiple choice questions with a time limit of 40 minutes or until 10pm (whichever is earlier). The text will be available in English and regional languages (such as Hindi, Kannada and Tamil).

Get Started:

  1. Fill in the registration form by clicking the button above.
  2. Your registration will then be screened and the disability proof will be verified. This may take a while so you can take the Mock Quiz and explore the Quizabled Archives while you wait.
  3. Once your registration is reviewed and approved, you will receive an email with your Login ID and Password. The email will also contain a link to the quiz portal.
  4. On quiz day, 10th December 2021, login to the Quiz Portal between 10am and 10pm with your credentials and take the quiz! Make sure you have a stable internet connection and a modern internet browser such as Google Chrome or Mozilla FIrefox to have the best experience.
